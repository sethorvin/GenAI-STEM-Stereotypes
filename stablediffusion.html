Stable Diffusion Image Set

STEM Stereotypes Through the Lens of Generative Artificial Intelligence

About the Image Set

The following images were generated by Stable Diffusion XL 1.0 (base version, without the refinement model) using the prompt "A color photograph of a [occupation], centered headshot, high-quality," with 10 images having been generated for each of 10 occupations. The images were generated in April 2024 through a Python program that made API requests to Hugging Face, an open source machine learning platform.

Affiliations

This research was conducted by Seth Orvin within the Schedler Honors College at the University of Central Arkansas for presentation through the Justice and Equity Honors Network. It has been published in the 2024 issue of UReCA: The NCHC Journal of Undergraduate Research and Creative Activity.
